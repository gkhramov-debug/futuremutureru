Privacy Policy

Futuremutureru / Content Factory is a demo integration used to showcase multi-platform publishing (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, etc.). We keep data collection to the minimum necessary to demonstrate OAuth login and content publishing flows.

What we collect

  • OAuth profile identifiers and access tokens returned by platforms (e.g., TikTok) strictly to perform the requested actions (login, draft creation, or publishing during tests).
  • Basic technical logs (timestamps, request/response status codes) for debugging and security.

How we use data

  • Authenticate the user, verify connection to the selected platform, and call the respective API on the user’s behalf.
  • Improve stability, investigate errors and abuse.

Data sharing & retention

  • We never sell personal data.
  • Access tokens are stored securely and rotated/invalidated on request.
  • Demo/test data may be deleted at any time on request: support@futuremutureru.example.

Your rights

You may request deletion of your data and revocation of granted permissions at any time. You can also revoke access via the platform’s settings (e.g., TikTok settings > Apps & websites).

Contact

For privacy questions: support@futuremutureru.example
